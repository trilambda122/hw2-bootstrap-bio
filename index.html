ABOUT ME

I am a telecommunications operations executive with a track record of starting up new operations, turning around underperforming operations, and increasing year-over-year revenue. I am skilled in delivering complex projects on time and within budget. I am a value-driven leader who promotes a collaborative and adaptable environment that rapidly adapts to changing market demands. I have attended the executive management program at Sloan School of Business, Thayer executive leadership program at West Point, been a member of the board of directors for several non-profits, and been published in telecommunications trade magazines.
