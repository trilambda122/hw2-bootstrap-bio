Bio

Shane has more than 20 years of versatile experience launching and strengthening technology operations across established and startup environments in the telecommunications and cable industries. Currently as CTO, he drives operational excellence and revenue growth of LOGIX’s enterprise-only Network & IT Systems delivering voice and data services. Previously, Shane championed business turnaround as VP of Technical Operations at Grande Communications, where he positioned the organization as a market leader in data, voice video, and data speed. He achieved similar profitable outcomes at Atlantic Broadband, including steering his team through merger and cultural changes following its acquisition of Charter Communications properties as Director of Network Engineering. Shane’s integral leadership during his tenure directly contributed to enhanced customer satisfaction, new technology implementation, and company growth from zero to 300,000 customers.

A mission-driven executive, Shane strives to cultivate dedicated work cultures focused on attaining overall business objectives while remaining adaptable to rapidly changing market demands. Shane holds certifications in Executive Leadership from MIT Sloan School of Business and West Point. Committed to continuous learning, he enjoys empowering others through ongoing training and mentorship.
